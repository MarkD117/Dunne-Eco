About us

Dunne Eco has a legacy spanning over 100 years, crafting excellence in the joinery trade.

About Dunne Eco

We strive to work in a culture of co-operation and trust with a focus on the needs of the customer. We have a wealth of expertise and knowledge in managing even the most complex projects - driven by an experienced, skilled team. Regardless of scale, we can manage your project from concept to completion.

Projects of Any Scale

From bespoke home projects to large developments, we bring expertise and attention to detail every step of the way.

Unmatched Craftsmanship

Quality You Can Trust

Our Services

From personalized consultations and precise onsite measurements to expertly crafted doors, windows, and stairs, we deliver tailored joinery solutions every step of the way.

Consultation

Feel free to contact us or call into us for a free consultation about any of our products and we will try our best to help find a workable solution.

Measure

Once you are happy with a product and a design, we can personally measure everything that we need to get the best fit for you.

Construction

After everything has been measured and designs have been picked out, construction of your door, window or stairs will begin.

Doors

We offer a wide range of doors, from veneer to solid wood or PVC and aluminium, we've got you covered for all of your needs.

Windows

We also provide a variety of tilt & turn PVC, aluminium and steel windows. If you'd prefer something more traditional we also offer sash windows.

Stairs

If you have another floor, we can get you there! We have an array of available stairs wooden and steel in a multitude of different styles.
